Technical Details

Extractive Summarization

  • Uses TF-IDF and sentence ranking algorithms
  • Preserves original wording and style
  • Fast processing and high accuracy
  • Best for factual content and reports

Abstractive Summarization

  • Powered by transformer neural networks
  • Generates new, coherent sentences
  • Better context understanding
  • Ideal for creative and complex texts
